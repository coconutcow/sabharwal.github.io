A journey around Mother Earth

A trove of my choicest moments from the travels I have made. I had a count in my mind to complete 30 countries before I turn 30. Due to some global restrictions on travelling, I changed that goal to 31 countries before I turn 31. But this all started as a joke, and now when I am about to complete them I have evolved as a person. My thoughts don’t connect with the San from the past. My lifestyle, my choices, my purposes (mukti), have changed. I happened to learn how life across many facets of planet Earth are nearly the same. We are all the same. Just a microcosm in a macrocosm. The ancient sages are probably right about the concept of the Maya - everything comes and goes, it is nothing but mere different names (naam), forms (roop), and behaviors (vyvhara), playing through the many dimensions they are in, and exhibit multiple frequencies to create mental perceptions in their spectators (us) - but at the core of it, we are all the same, we are all one (aham brahmaasmi). Ok - enough of philosphy! Scroll down, and glance through my memory lane.
