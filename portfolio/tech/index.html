Title: The Illusion of Happiness

Description: A datascience project where the objective is to question recent reports that rank countries by their Happiness Index. The factors that are usually utilized by these reports are economic - but when we look closer into data parameters from another perspective of what might happiness be, the results are in fact the complete opposite. The wealthiest countries have a poor ’life’ track record. Not a project to challenge any authority - but rather make one think, on what is Happiness ? and should it be our objective ? or should we seek Peace ? You decide :)
